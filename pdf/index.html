Recommendation Notes

  1. Option 1: Best for institutional donors and foundation stakeholders; strongly donor-focused with annual-report credibility.
  2. Option 2: Best for education partners, museums, and cultural organizations; most educational/editorial in tone.
  3. Option 3: Best for digital campaigns and fundraising landing pages; most conversion-focused while remaining dignified.
  4. Option 4: Best when continuity with the printed tri-fold is important; balanced donor + educational communication.
  5. Option 5: Best for memorial events and high-touch major donor outreach; most emotional and prestige-oriented.
